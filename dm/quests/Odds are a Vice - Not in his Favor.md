## Premise
[[Vice Murglen]] - dark violet skinned tiefling and a former member of [[The Stallignment]]. He's jumpy, paranoid, and rightly so...
			- Prominent "fixer" maybe 10-15 years ago back in [[New Sigil]], prominently going between the crime syndicate and [[The Kismet]] before essentially working between everyone and [[The Ascendent]].
			- Intel: If his debts are paid off (6500gp), he's prepared to share the keyword to safe docking at [[Devil's Bargain]]. 
				- Trends: if you act like you belong there and flex your strength, you won't get bothered too much. But any signs of visible weakness, including others offering deference as an act, can get met with a challenger. 
				- E.g., Where you going w/ your captured hands that you can't go by yourself? Maybe someone else attests to needing them more

Half of the [[Casino Orinoco]] is under the thumb of [[The Stallignment]], in particular [[Kreevah the Greedy]] who runs the floating casino.

Kreevah and his chief body guard [[Clurb the Troll]] are aware of [[Vice Murglen]]'s position as "on shaky ground." But they are generally unaware that a bartender [[Tzimira Malatrik]] has an active bounty out to take [[Vice Murglen]] out - forever. He's a liability and knows "too much" - and the degree of his treachery has led some to think he's a member of [[The Riggers]].

## Setup

The assassination of [[Vice Murglen]] is in-progress the moment the party steps on board. After he's killed, the [[Kreevah the Greedy]] offers a prize (500gp or full 5d20 wheel spin) to anyone willing to solve the case and ID the culprit. 

Reverse spins on **Talravi's Fallacy** - see [[Session 34]] - can roll back time up to 3 times a voyage.
## Series of Events

At the table:
1. [[Vice Murglen]] wins a massive pot playing Skink's Business (~1500gp) and yells out: *Finally!! I've done it! I've got the money!!
2. A group of interested but innocent bystanders (6 others) rush him to see how much he's money he raked in
3. Vice quietly messages [[Clurb the Troll]] for an escort to his quarters on the West Wing.
	1. Uh - Clurb...Clurb! Please, an escort as I retire to my quarters. Get me out of here and make it seem real serious like. I don't want to be followed!
4. Clurb arrives and says:
	1. If you'll come with me  right this way Mr Murglen, the House would like a word with you.
## Reward
Goal is to either: 
- Figure out who killed [[Vice Murglen]], for a lump reward of 500gp from the house...Or one free, full spin on the wheel 
- Intervene and "save" Vice to live for another day - extract some of his intel
