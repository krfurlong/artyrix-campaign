With a low grinding noise, you all collectively look up to see the large wall of ice and stone sliding open to reveal the heart of the iceberg. The smell hits you first: of decay, of blood, a certain dry staleness to the air. Beyond the passage way, the cavern opened up slightly to reveal an enormous chamber glinting with what you presume to be ice. ## Combat with Zostym

DE-ESCALATION ROLLS.
LAIR Actions (20):
- Splash Ice Pools: DC13 Con save or take 1d10 cold damage, half damage on save, if within 5ft from pool
- Drop icicles: DC13 dex save or take 2d6 piercing damage (fun or none) from one falling icicle in a 10ft cube
- Summon Icy whirlwind: DC13 con save or take 1d10 cold. All clouds move 30ft in a direction of Zostym's choosing
- Sorrowful Wail: DC15 Wisdom save or immediately fall prone overwhelmed by sadness of the young dragon

### Deescalating the fight
Skill check within the initiative order - 3 rounds of "successes" before 3 rounds of "failures" will wind combat down.

Base DC: 16.
- Each PC that uses their action to "de-escalate" by doing something reasonable lowers the DC by 1.
- Hostile/aggressive things done each round increase the DC by 2.
- Each PC rolls for themselves. Majority of success/fails each round determine the success overall.
