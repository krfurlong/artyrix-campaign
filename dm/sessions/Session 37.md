### Parallel Vessel of [[The Kismet]]
As the day progresses, it slowly but very apparently begins to dawn on all of you that you must be nearing the city. While fog off the port-side shrouds where you presume there must be land, you grow increasingly aware that you are not alone on these waters. Ships of every size, shape, and bearing colors of every recognizable faction gradually percolate around you on all sides. By no intention or planning on your part, you've fallen into the steady stream of vessels all filtering into and out of the incredibly enormous city that awaits beyond the horizon.

Some ships:
- A new vessel bearing the colors of [[The New Transit]], seemingly heavily laden with some sort of cargo and drifting slowly towards the city 
	- bringing stone/bricks into the city to drive the skyline higher
- An aquatic chariot-pontoon pulled by a posse of dolphins, steered by a triton couple
	- Returning from a dual-mission from the [[The Defiant]] and [[The Open Hand]] to debrief from being called in to provide aid and counseling services to a group of kuo-toa off the shores of [[Pythinia]] - seemingly convinced they had seen a god soar over them
- One vessel in particular catches your eye - [[Bugsy]] - a low lying vessel (ironclad) with oars puncturing its sides is deep into the water save a small exposed deck on the top of the vessel upon which three figures stand attentive and defensive. The banner of a fist gripping an hourglass flares angrily in the wind - a clear sign of a vessel of [[The Kismet]].
