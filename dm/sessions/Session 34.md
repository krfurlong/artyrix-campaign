## Intro
Catching the shifting tides, you all cast off from shore as the sun breaks though the patchy cloudy sky. Aoife you steer your ship to catch some receding waves, kicking up a fine briny mist that your sloop cuts through like a hot knife through butter. It's refreshing - no, liberating - to be back on the open seas once more. A burst of exhilaration pulses through you as you carve your modest vessel northward. A rescue operation - wrapped in a shroud of mystery - truly the stuff of great legends and rote songs of yore.

Exiting from Aoife's perspective, however, the clouds of the sky take on a bit more ominous and deceptive sheen. Kai's brother seems very much alive - and with a general location even known - though fallen into the clutches of a group far from savory or friendly. Words of advise and prophecy from [[Hounga Kalfou]] and [[Soneela]] respectively weigh heavy in the minds of Kryst, Sybil, and Bugsy, most sharply - as you grapple with information about yourselves and your next steps. Truths, lies, half-truths, and half-lies, swirling together like the sea spume and flotsam that you sail through now.

*The day - and the high seas - are yours.*
