## Intro
As you take a short rest in the midday warmth, you all note the forest features that surround you are familiar and yet different - as if exaggerated in color and stature from the trees that you passed by earlier. The change isn't so much unwelcome or threatening as it is unsettling. Things are familiar and yet different - birdsong is both softer and yet more distinct. A crispness to the air around you cuts through and shocks your lungs despite the overall dankness and humid weight of the somewhat swampy environment around you. Your short rest ends as you arise and trek back towards the fork in the trail - retracing your steps to where you first lost sight of the tracks of the youth lost in these strange woods.
