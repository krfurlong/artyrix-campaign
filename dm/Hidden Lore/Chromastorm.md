A "rival" adventuring party to the [[Soleless Sailors]].
They sail upon the [[The Starmaiden]].

	- battle hardened slayer of Remorhaz's in northern [[Linnæsa]]. Has seen some really nasty shit and a bit of a grizzled war vet because of it. Plenty of scar stories.
- Bugbear barbarian - Bubo, wild magic
	- Allegedly escaped from some pretty heinous experiments being conducted in [[Delta Prime]]. He doesn't want to talk about it but enters a trance state if provoked.
- wild magic female sorcerer drow - Umbria
	- Close friend of Bubo before and after the incident in [[Delta Prime]].
	- A bit more level-headed and adept as calming Bubo down.
	- They both hail from the humanoid enclave on [[Delta Minor]]
- Life domain loxodon female - Rarjan Pestradoxa, from [[Harazid]] on [[Dynistia]], knows/is aware of [[Sybil]]'s family
- Dark gray, Owlin rogue - Skreek. Criminal history breaking and entering, burglary in the upper districts of [[New Sigil]].
