## Bracers of the Silverspirit Dragon (To-Be)

## The Cinder Cortex (in-progress)

## Acidspire Halbeard
Made from the talon of a copper dragon
## Quiver of Brasshorn
- Bonus action to add addition 2d6 lightning damage to single attack; 
- Next person who attacks gets advantage (cannot be same person)

## Quicksilver Satchel
Made from one of the throat glands of a great **mercury** dragon
- can use a bonus action to throw a 20, 30, or 40 foot line of quicksilver that immediate solidifies; 
- one can use their movement speed along the line of mercury unimpeded by terrain/altitude

## Iron Shield of the Hunter
- Adv on Survival Checks; 
- +1 Magic Shield;
- Bonus action to make shove attack
made with the center-scale of an iron dragon

## The Dual Talonsword
Gave two talons from right claw to a trusted friend and great fighter before the two of them went into heated combat with a shared foe – Teorsorg The Soaring Cinder;

2 handed weapon; 
+2 magic item;
1d12 damage + 2;
- DC15 Con Save or take additional 1d6 bleeding damage at start of next turn;
	- Remake the save at the end of the turn
	- Must succeed or use action to stem bleeding; 
	- extra 1d6 per failure - Stacks but success heals all wounds

## Cape of the Valiant
Allegedly sewn of the wings of Marduk the Platinum – not willingly given.
- Fly speed 40ft;
- +2 to AC;
- Adv vs spell saves;
