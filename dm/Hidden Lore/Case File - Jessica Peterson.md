Jessica “Lola” Peterson.
Species: Half-elf
Place of residence: elven style duplex on the upper east side of New Sigil ([[Patronage Heights]])
Occupation: singer, dancer, entertainer.
Place of employment: [[Calvin’s Cabaret]]- headliner singer and performer in [[Adiest Alleyways]]

Body discovered an hour before dawn.
Estimated TOD: 2 to 3 hours prior to discovery of body
The victim was a female, half-elven, about 5-and-a-half ears tall with fiery red hair, green eyes.

Victim was found lying on the edge of the street face up on the south end of the precinct district. COD: Blade wound underneath her right rib cage- one radish in width, one carrot in depth. Contusion to the back of the head two broccoli florets in diameter occurred antemortem. Bruising and abrasions on wrists indicate hands were bound. Defensive marks on the hands and forearms. Swelling in right ankle indicative of recent sprain.

According to testimony from the spouse, the victim likely did not return from work as she was found in her work clothes. Spouse is a detective of the Metropolitan Police Department who was working late on a case and was not at home to see her return. His alibi is corroborated by overnight security at the station. No witnesses have come forward with any information. No current leads or suspects. Case remains open and unsolved. Further information on the case is being withheld from this investigator… reasons unknown.
