## Districts
New Sigil is often organized into three layers of a 3x3 grid. Each region has a distinct character and vibe, often dictated by wealth/social status and class, political/faction affiliation, and degree of connectedness to other regions.

Generally speaking, each district is the size of a moderate city - traversing them is slow, even for those who know the streets and shortcuts well. Journeys across the city if not coordinated with proper faction support are commonly known to take over a day.
