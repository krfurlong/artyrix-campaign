#place 
Region in [[New Sigil]].

## Description
A metallic and art deco inspired region along the western coast of New Sigil. The guilding in the area are bronze, brass, and gilded gold - featuring floral patters that twist and twirl like ivy along the walls of the buildings. Windows and glass are both plentiful, causing the entire local area to be comparatively more warm and humid relative to the rest of the city. While a coastal wind breaks through occasionally, the norm is a greenhouse-like heat and a hustle/bustle of merchants and long-haulers coming and going among the streets that seem to never sleep.
