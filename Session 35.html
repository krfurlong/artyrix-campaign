Session 35

Date: 3/1/2026
Title:

Recap

  • Party sailed north from Havangart/Rifterra
  • Met and interacted with Chromastorm, largely to neutral interactions
  • They found the Casino Orinoco, interacted with various NPCs onboard
  • Witnessed the heinous murder of Vice Murglen, and then were thrust into a means to save his life and solve the crime

Intro

With a sudden lurch of realization, you all almost collectively and simultaneously break from the distorted cycle from which you were trapped in: Singling out the drow bartender Tzimira Malatrik as the presumed culprit behind the assassination of Vice Murglen. Collecting evidence from across the Casino Orinoco - debunking red herrings and winnowing down the list of suspects - You moved swiftly on this final rotation triggered by Talravi's Fallacy - taunted by Kreevah the Greedy all the while. As time shifts into a new trajectory, Kai - you find yourself face to face with Tzimira, crouched menacingly behind the doorway inside Clurb the Troll's room, uttering with a chillingly calm and lack of emotion tone that she's had it with your attempts to stand the in way of her and her quarry, as she draws a long blade of pure shadow from seemingly out of the darkness beside her and prepares to strike.

Back on the Seas

Kobold Boarding Party

The night watch starts peacefully as the moon reflects sporadically across the choppy waters of the Eternal Sea.

Perception DC13 - As the hours stretch on, an odd shape appears in the water - slightly bobbing up and down. It's only upon getting closer to it that you recognize it as ice - this piece maybe about a foot across in size and certainly not large enough damage your ship.

DC18 --> to each side of you, you also see two larger pieces of what you think to be ice. There are larger and mostly submerged but unlike the errant small bergs in front of you, these seem to move on their own accord - and they're swiftly moving in your direction, vectoring on a curiously precise intercept path ~100 feet away.

Perception DC14: A thump from the back of your shift, as a single cloaked figure rises, they are tall and lanky
("scary/fake" name: Tundrax the Arbiter)
real name: (chief): Zamboni;
torso kobold is: Icktray
feet is: ick-pick
They've been sent on a vision quest by Zumaj to retrieve the artifact held by Krystabald -- they are certainly Zumaji through and through.

Hello fair adventurer. I am Tundrax, the arbiter. And I am charged by our great draconic librarian in the Ruby Palace and come to relieve you of that which burdens you. I speak of course of Zumaj - the Viridescent Archivist bids those in our order to act when faced with those who would sit on knowledge and use the gifts that Zumaj provides to advance their astral will.

Take comfort, lowly inventor: you have chances to redeem yourself. But until that time, Zumaj requires that we extricate the gauntlet you wear. Time is impossibly short for us here in Artyrix - and Zumaj grows wearing of your trifling inventions that do not advance discovery and creation itself.

Join us - we aim to strike at the heart of a silver dragon (young silver dragon) in it's lair but a days journey northwest of here. Prove yourself there and perhaps the Drake of Knowledge Lost will deem your soul worthy of their unmeasurable power and talon-tinged artifacts. Fail yet again to act upon the pursuit of the creation of knowledge, and we will be forced to separate you from gauntlet. Impede us there and our Divine Drake shall surely strike you asunder.
